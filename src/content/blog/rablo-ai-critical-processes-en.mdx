In enterprise operations, bottlenecks are rarely just technical.  
They are usually slow flows, poor traceability, and scattered approvals.

That's where **Rablo** comes in.

## Context

I currently contribute to Rablo from **CompuSoluciones y Asociados**, building capabilities to digitize critical processes.

## My role in this project

**Product development at CompuSoluciones.**

## Problems it addresses

- Slow authorization flows.
- Dependency on informal channels.
- Limited visibility into process status.
- Operational compliance risk.

## Product direction

- Standardized critical workflows.
- Better decision traceability.
- AI integration to speed up operational tasks.
- A platform usable by business teams.

## Expected outcome

Higher speed, stronger control, and better auditability in daily operations.
