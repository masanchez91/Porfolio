**Run Territory** is the next step in my path as a product builder.

It is not an improvised experiment.  
It is a strategic bet built around product, community, and iterative execution.

## Why it exists

I want to build an experience that combines:

- Real value for a specific community.
- Simplicity from day one.
- Architecture ready to scale.

## My role

**Strategic definition, architecture design, and full-stack technical execution.**

## Product principles

1. Launch fast, but with criteria.
2. Measure behavior before scaling.
3. Prioritize retention over vanity metrics.
4. Turn feedback into an actionable roadmap.

## Initial roadmap (Q1-Q2 2026)

- Phase 1: Functional MVP with core flow.
- Phase 2: Validation with first users.
- Phase 3: Onboarding and engagement improvements.
- Phase 4: Iterated public release.

## What I will share here

In this blog I will publish:

- Product decisions.
- Technical learnings.
- Early metrics and pivots.
- What works (and what doesn't).

Run Territory is not trying to look good in a pitch.  
It is aiming to become a product people truly want to use.
