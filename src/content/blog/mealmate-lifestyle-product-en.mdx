MealMate+ starts from a simple idea:  
if daily habits are disconnected, progress is disconnected too.

## Problem we targeted

- Fragmented planning (food, routine, habits).
- Low consistency due to too much friction.
- Generic recommendations without user context.

## My role

**Strategic definition, architecture design, and full-stack technical execution.**

## Solution

- Unified lifestyle tracking experience.
- Personalization through intelligent recommendations.
- Interface focused on continuity and daily clarity.

## Outcome

- **500+ active users**.
- Strong base for iterations and deeper personalization layers.

## Key lesson

In consumer products, retention does not depend only on features.  
It depends on creating an experience users want to repeat.
