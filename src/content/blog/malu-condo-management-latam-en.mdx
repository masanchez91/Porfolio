The condominium management sector has a clear pattern:  
manual processes, fragmented communication, and reactive operations.

**Malu** was built to centralize and simplify that chaos.

## Core market pain

- Poor traceability in collections.
- Unreliable access control.
- Amenity booking conflicts.
- Fragmented communication between admins and residents.

## My role

**Strategic definition, architecture design, and full-stack technical execution.**

## What we solved

- Digital collections and operational follow-up.
- QR-validated access control.
- Booking system with clear rules and visibility.
- Management dashboard with key metrics.

## Impact

- Up to **80% reduction** in administrative operational load.
- Better financial clarity and less user friction.

## Product lesson

In vertical B2B, winning is not about adding more features.  
It is about designing the right workflow for users' daily reality.
