If an SMB runs support on WhatsApp, losing speed means losing sales.  
With **Wavi**, the goal was simple: automate repetitive work without losing human tone.

## The challenge

- High volume of repetitive questions.
- Limited support hours.
- Inconsistent conversation follow-up.
- Sales cooling down due to response time.

## My role

**Strategic definition, architecture design, and full-stack technical execution.**

## Product solution

- Automated responses for frequent questions.
- Fast setup for non-technical teams.
- Structured flow to quote and move conversations forward.
- Centralized tracking for commercial follow-up.

## Operational outcome

- Up to **60% of conversations automated**.
- **24/7 support** without increasing headcount.
- Better first-response time and improved conversion.

## Most important insight

Automation works when it is designed for business outcomes, not just message replies.  
Wavi is built to move conversations toward close.
